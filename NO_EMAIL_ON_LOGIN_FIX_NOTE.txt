NO EMAIL APP ON LOGIN FIX

Problem:
The generic website form handler was catching login/register/dashboard forms.
When Formspree was not configured, it opened a pre-filled email fallback.

Fix:
- revnrip-site.js now ignores Supabase-controlled forms.
- Login/register forms are handled only by revnrip-auth.js.
- Dashboard bounty form is handled only by Supabase.
- Message forms are handled only by revnrip-chat.js.
- Contact/sell/grail forms still use Formspree or email fallback as intended.

Affected protected form IDs:
- loginForm
- registerForm
- bountyForm
- startMessageForm
- messageComposer
